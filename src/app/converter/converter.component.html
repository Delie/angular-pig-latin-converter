Conversion Rules:

  • If the word starts with a vowel (A/E/I/O/U) then 'way' is appended to the end of the word.
  • If the word starts with a consonant, and also contains A/E/I/O/U (or Y, when not the first letter), then all consonants before the first vowel are moved to the end of the word. In addition, 'ay' is appended to theend of the word.
